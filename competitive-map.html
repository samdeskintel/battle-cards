Top competitive landmines
Alerts solved detection years ago — the challenge now is decision speed.
Most teams already have alerts. What they lack is fast understanding.
Analysts spend most of their time validating alerts, not making decisions.
One incident often generates dozens of alerts analysts must consolidate.
Strategic intelligence helps planning. Operations need real-time decisions.
Human intelligence cannot scale globally in real time.
Communication platforms assume the problem is already understood.
Mass notifications are rare. Most incidents require targeted operational decisions.
Forecasting risk is different from understanding events as they unfold.
The future of security operations is automation, not dashboards.
If analysts spend hours interpreting alerts, the system works for the software.
Winning platforms will turn signals into decisions automatically.
